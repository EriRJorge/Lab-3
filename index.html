About J's Pizza

J's Pizza has been a beloved fixture in our community for 32 years, founded by John and Jane Smith in 1991. This family-owned pizzeria has earned its reputation by consistently delivering delicious, locally sourced pizzas made with the freshest ingredients. Their menu has evolved to offer a wide range of options, from classic Margherita to unique creations like BBQ Chicken and Pesto Veggie, along with appetizers, salads, and pasta dishes to cater to every taste. What sets J's Pizza apart is not just their mouthwatering food, but also their warm and friendly atmosphere. Whether you're a regular or a first-time visitor, you'll be greeted with a smile and treated like family. Their commitment to community engagement, including sponsoring local sports teams and participating in charity events, has endeared them even more to their customers. As they celebrate their 32nd anniversary, J's Pizza continues to passionately serve exceptional pizzas and create unforgettable dining experiences, solidifying their position as a cherished institution in our community for years to come.

32 Years of Expertise

Jonathan Gyves, the visionary founder of J's Pizza, embarked on a culinary journey that began over three decades ago. With an unwavering passion for authentic Italian flavors and an unyielding commitment to quality, Jonathan, together with his partner Jane Smith, brought their dream of sharing exceptional pizza to life in 1991. His dedication to sourcing the freshest ingredients, perfecting recipes, and fostering a welcoming atmosphere has been the cornerstone of J's Pizza's enduring success. Jonathan's leadership and community-oriented spirit have not only made J's Pizza a beloved local institution but also a symbol of his culinary legacy, proving that a true commitment to excellence can stand the test of time.
